    Network Devices
    Router
    Routes data between 
different networks,
typically connecting 
a local network to 
the internet.
    
Hub 
    A basic device that 
broadcasts data to all
devices on a network,
without intelligent 
data forwarding.
    
Bridge
    Connects two separate 
networks and filters
traffic between them,
reducing network traffic.
    
Wireless Router 
    It Combines a router and
a wireless access point,
providing both wired
and wireless internet 
connectivity.
    
Switch  
    It directs data to specific
devices on a network by 
learning their MAC addresses,
improving network 
efficiency.
    
Wireless Bridge
    It Connects two or more
networks wirelessly, 
extending the range 
of a wired network.
