    Introduction to Networking
      Networking can refer to
  the process of building 
  relationships and making 
  connections, or to the
  practice of connecting 
  devices to share resources 
  and data.
    Networking allows for: 
     Resource sharing
    Sharing devices like printers, 
files, and storage across
multiple computers,reducing
costs and improving efficiency.
    
Improved communication
    Facilitating easy communication 
between individuals and teams 
through email, instant messaging, 
and video conferencing.
    
Centralized data management
    Storing and accessing data from
a central location,enabling easier
data backup and consistency. 
    
Information sharing
    Accessing and distributing knowledge 
and updates within an organization.
    
Business growth
    Expanding market reach and
collaborating with partners through
online networks.
    
Security enhancements
    Implementing security measures like 
firewalls and access controls to 
protect sensitive data.
