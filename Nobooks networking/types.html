    Types of Networking 
    LAN (Local Area Network)
    A network that connects devices
within a small geographic area, 
like a home or office.
    
WAN (Wide Area Network)
    A network that spans large 
geographic areas,connecting 
multiple LANs across cities, 
countries, or continents.
    
MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)
    A network that covers a city 
or large campus,typically larger
than a LAN but smaller than a WAN.
